ABOUT 'CSI STUDENT BRANCH'

 

Formed in 1964/65, the Computer Society of India (CSI) is a largest and most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India. The purposes of the Society are scientific and educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science and IT.

The organization has grown to an enviable size of 100,000 strong members consisting of professionals with varied backgrounds including Software developers, Scientists, Academicians, Project Managers, CIO’s, CTO’s & IT vendors to just name a few. It has spread its branches all over the country. Currently has more than 500 student branches and rooted firmly at 73 different locations

The CSI Student Branch at IPEC was incepted in September 2017 under the aegis of Department of Computer Science. It is an official student body with 123 motivated student members of IPEC. The benefits and value of CSI membership can be explored from www.csi-india.org. Some of the key benefits are:

        • Participation in workshops, lectures, seminars, conferences, quiz contests, programming competitions, at chapter/regional/national level.
        • Participate in Certification Training & Skill development programs at subsidized rates
        • Funding for student projects up to Rs. 5000
        • Participation in events with special discounts.
        • Award for students for outstanding performance in the student branch
        • Guidance from academia/Industry Luminaries for better career opportunities
        • Opportunity to publish papers in CSI Adhyayan, with prize-winning opportunities
        • Gain access to Emerging Industry trends

Publication with CSI- Students can publish their original articles with following journals.

        • CSI Communications in Print, Digital, and an App form.
        • CSI Journal of Computing
        • CSI Transactions on ICT

The CSI student branch at IPEC is managed by the student committee under the guidance of faculty-student branch counselor. The committee organizes a number of events including programming contests, talks by renowned speakers, workshops etc. which give the students an exposure to the competitive computing world as well as allow them to understand the advancements going on in the computing sphere worldwide.

IPEC CSI Student Branch is working on four Special Interest Groups (SIGs) Java, Android, Robotic and Web Due to subject-specific nature; the student chapter invariably focuses on information and insights of the relevant fields.
